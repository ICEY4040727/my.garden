# 1.$\displaystyle\int\frac{1+\cos x+\sin x}{1+\sin^2 x}\ d\ x$

$\displaystyle=\int\frac{1}{1+\sin^2 x}\ d\ x+\int\frac{\cos x}{1+\sin^2 x}\ dx+\int\frac{\sin x}{1+\sin^2 x}\ dx$

$\displaystyle=\int\frac{1}{2\sin^2 x+\cos^2 x}\ dx+\int\frac{1}{1+\sin^2 x}\ d\sin x-\int\frac{1}{2-\cos^2 x}\ d\cos x$

$\displaystyle=\int\frac{1}{2\tan^2 x+1}\ d\tan x+\int\frac{1}{1+\sin^2 x}\ d\sin x-\int\frac{1}{2-\cos^2 x}\ d\cos x$
