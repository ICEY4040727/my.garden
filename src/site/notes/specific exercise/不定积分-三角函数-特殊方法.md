# 1.二倍角公式
{ #b945bc}


## 1.$\displaystyle\int \frac{1}{1+\cos x}\ dx$

$\displaystyle= \int\frac{1}{2(\cos{\frac{x}{2}})^2}\ dx$

$\displaystyle=\int(\sec \frac{x}{2})^2\ d\ \frac{x}{2}$

$\displaystyle=\tan\frac{x}{2}+C$

## 2.$\displaystyle\int\sin^4 x\cos^2 x\ d\ x$

$\displaystyle=\frac{1}{4}\int(\frac{1- \cos 2x}{2})\cdot\sin^2 2x\ d\ x$

$\displaystyle=\frac{1}{8}\int\sin^2 2x\ dx\ +\ \frac{1}{8}\int\cos 2x\cdot\sin^2 2x\ dx$

$\displaystyle=\frac{1}{16}\int1-\cos 2x\ dx\ +\ \frac{1}{16}\int\sin^2 2x\ d\ 2x$


# 2.按照对称方法凑积分变量
{ #dcb34e}


## 1.若$R(\sin x,-\cos x)=-R(\sin x,\cos x)$,则想办法凑出$\sin x$

### 1.$\displaystyle\int\frac{1}{\sin^2 x\cos x}\ dx$

>[!info] 上下同乘$\cos x凑出d\ \sin x$

$\displaystyle= \int\frac{\cos x}{\sin^2 x\cos^2 x}\ d\ x$

$\displaystyle= \int\frac{1}{\sin^2 x(1-\sin^2 x)} \ d\ \sin x$

$\displaystyle =\int\frac{1}{t^2(1-t^2)}\ d\ t$   

>[!info] *[[specific exercise/不定积分-有理分式-裂项#^2daebd\|共轭裂项]]*

$\displaystyle=\int\frac{1}{t^2}+\frac{1}{1-t^2}\ d\ t$


### 2.$\displaystyle\int \sec^3 x\ dx$

$\displaystyle=\int\frac{\cos x}{\cos^4 x}\ dx$

$\displaystyle =\int \frac{1}{(1-\sin^2 x)^2}\ d\ \sin x$

$\displaystyle=\int\frac{1}{(1-t^2)^2}\ d\ t$

到这一步就又化归成我们配凑过的样子

>[!info] [[specific exercise/不定积分-有理分式-配凑#^be3592]\|配凑]]


## 2.若$R(-\sin x,\cos x)=-R(\sin x,\cos x)$,则想办法凑出$\cos x$

### 1.$\displaystyle\int\frac{1}{\sin x\cdot\cos^2 x}\ dx$

$\displaystyle = -\int\frac{1}{\sin^2 x\cdot\cos^2 x}\ d\cos x$

$\displaystyle= -\int\frac{1}{(1-t^2)t^2}\ d\ t$

接下来参考[[specific exercise/不定积分-有理分式-裂项#^2daebd\|共轭裂项]]

### 2.$\displaystyle\int\frac{5+4\cos x}{\sin x(2+\cos x)^2}\ dx$

$\displaystyle=- \int \frac{5+4\cos x}{\sin^2 x\ (2+\cos x)^2}\ d\ \cos x$


## 3.若$R(-\sin x,-\cos x)=-R(\sin x,\cos x)$,则想办法凑出$\tan x$

### 1.$\displaystyle \int \frac{1}{1+\cos^2 x}\ d\ x$

$\displaystyle=\int \frac{1}{1+\sec^2 x}\ d\ \tan x$

$\displaystyle=\int\frac{1}{\tan^2 x+2}\ d\ \tan x$


### 2.$\displaystyle\int\frac{1}{(3\sin x+2\cos x)^2}\ d\ x$

$\displaystyle=\int \frac{\sec^2 x}{(3\tan x+2)^2}\ d\ x$

$\displaystyle=\int\frac{1}{(3\tan x+2)^2}\ d\ \tan x$
