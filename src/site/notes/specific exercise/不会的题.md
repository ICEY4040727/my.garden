#### $\displaystyle\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\sin x \ln\sin x\ d\ x$

$\displaystyle= -\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \ln \sin x \, d\cos x$

$=\displaystyle -\cos x \cdot \ln \sin x \bigg|_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} + \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \frac{1 - \sin^2 x}{\sin x} \, dx$

$=\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 0^+} \ln x + \ln|\csc x - \cot x| \bigg|_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} + \cos x \bigg|_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}$

$=\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 0^+} \ln x + \ln 2 - \lim_{x \to 0^+} \ln\frac{x}{2} - 1$

$\displaystyle= \ln 2 - 1$
